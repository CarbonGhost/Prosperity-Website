## Bell Chain Ringing

When a bell is connected to a set of chains the player can interact with the chain to ring the bell.

(source: [Supplementaries](https://modrinth.com/mod/supplementaries)

## Placeable Gunpowder

Gunpowder trails can be placed on the ground and be lit up by fire and fire arrows. Once the gunpowder trail is lit, it will consume itself in outwards from the source. Lit trails can ignite TNT, set flammable blocks on fire, or light up blocks like campfires or candles.

(source: [Supplementaries](https://modrinth.com/mod/supplementaries))

# More

## Cauldrons

Cauldrons can now store any liquid and can now be used to mix potions and dye. When a fire source is placed beneath a cauldron, the contents will begin to boil, dealing damage when an entity stands in the cauldron.

(source: [Amendments](https://modrinth.com/mod/amendments))

## XP Bottling

When shift clicking an Enchanting Table with a bottle or jar you can bottle some experience at the cost of health.

(source: [Supplementaries](https://modrinth.com/mod/supplementaries))

## Zombie Horse Conversion

Feeding a stack of rotten flesh to a owned skeleton horse will slowly convert it into a zombie horse. Additionally, zombie horses can breath underwater just like skeleton horses.

(source: [Supplementaries](https://modrinth.com/mod/supplementaries))
